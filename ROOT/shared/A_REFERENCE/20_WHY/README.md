# 20_WHY - Purpose [BASE]

> **5W2H - WHY:** Why does the entity exist and act this way? Purpose, mission, values, decisions (the deep 'why').

## What goes here
- The **purpose** and reason-for-being (timeless).
- **Mission, vision, values, principles** that guide conduct.
- **Objectives** (qualitative / OKR) and the **value proposition**.
- **Decision rationale** (ADRs): the *argument* behind a decision.

## What does NOT go here
- The **dated fact** of a decision (when it was taken) -> `12_STATUS_Daily` (linked, not duplicated; P1).
- Quantitative targets/KPIs -> `75_HOWMUCH_Metrics`; dated milestones -> `65_WHEN_Milestones`.
- Operating policies/rules -> `37_HOW_Policies` / `03_CORE_Rules`.

## How to read the derivatives (laws)
1. **Canonical home + facets (P1)**: each thing has ONE home here; its other dimensions are read-only `facet:` pointers in the frontmatter, never copies.
2. **One catalogue (P5)**: the 4-letter code namespace lives in `05_CORE_Glossary`; trees here are *projections* of it.
3. **Plans emit facts (P3)**: lifecycle events are pointed at `12_STATUS_Daily`, not transcribed here.
4. **Generated index (P7)**: `index.html` / `index.json` are generated, not hand-edited.

## Derivatives
- `21_WHY_Purpose` - The ultimate reason the entity exists.
- `22_WHY_Mission` - What we do, permanently.
- `23_WHY_Vision` - The aspirational future, undated.
- `24_WHY_Values` - The values and beliefs the entity holds.
- `25_WHY_Principles` - Guiding operating principles.
- `26_WHY_Objectives` - Qualitative strategic objectives / OKRs.
- `27_WHY_ValueProposition` - The value proposition + why stakeholders matter.
- `28_WHY_Decisions` - Decision rationale / ADRs (the 'why' of each decision).
- `29_WHY_Impact` - The positive impact pursued.
