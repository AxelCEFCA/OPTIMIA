# 60_WHEN - Planning & future (the 'should') [BASE]

> **5W2H - WHEN:** When? Planned calendar, schedules, cadences, on-call, milestones, triggers, roadmap. The 'should', not the 'was'.

## What goes here
- **Planned** events (calendar), **schedules/rotas**, recurring **cadences**.
- **On-call** plans, future **milestones/deadlines**, **triggers**, the phased **roadmap**.

## What does NOT go here
- What actually **happened** -> `10_STATUS`. **A plan does not move when its date passes**: it EMITS a fact in STATUS and stays here as a cadence/commitment (P3).
- Reported effort/hours -> `74_HOWMUCH_Effort` / STATUS.

## How to read the derivatives (laws)
1. **Canonical home + facets (P1)**: each thing has ONE home here; its other dimensions are read-only `facet:` pointers in the frontmatter, never copies.
2. **One catalogue (P5)**: the 4-letter code namespace lives in `05_CORE_Glossary`; trees here are *projections* of it.
3. **Plans emit facts (P3)**: lifecycle events are pointed at `12_STATUS_Daily`, not transcribed here.
4. **Generated index (P7)**: `index.html` / `index.json` are generated, not hand-edited.
